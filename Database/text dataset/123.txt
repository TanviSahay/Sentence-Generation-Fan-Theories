Stark Season Approaching.

A fan explains that Robert Baratheon may have cursed the Starks in the first episode of the first season. All the Starks he touched — Ned, Catelyn, Robb, and Rickon — have died horrible deaths. But that's over now, and House Stark is back on the warpath. Also worth noting is that he asks Arya her name, which could explain her subsequent troubles.
