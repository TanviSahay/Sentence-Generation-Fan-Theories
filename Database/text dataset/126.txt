Jaime: Kingslayer, Kinslayer?

Now that Cersei is facing the possibility of a disastrous trial due to the High Sparrow's machinations, she could turn desperate. We already know that she has her spies investigating rumors, which might well be the presence of huge stores of wildfire under King's Landing. If she plans to burn the capital to the ground, that could put Jaime in a very awkward position. More precisely: the same position he was in when he killed the Mad King. We know how that turned out last time. This theory posits that history would repeat itself
